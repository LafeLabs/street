Trash Magic Flags

Trash magic flags are rainbow-on-black flags which point to domains on the Magic Dump.

To create a flag, cut out about a 3x3 foot square, hem all the edges, put trash ties in the hem to attach flag to things, and then cut out 4 inch squares from a sequence of colors of cloth that cycles through the rainbow and cut out letters for the domains and sittch them to the flag and wave the flag from a skeletron stick.

Flags are self-replicating media made from trash. Flags can be flown or hung in, on, and around the physical space that they represent.
